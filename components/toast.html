Toast

The toast component is used to show alerts on top of an overlay and gives feedback to users after an action has occured. The toast will close itself when the close button is clicked, or after a timeout â€” the default is 5 seconds.

Toast Usage

Warning: This is a warning alert 
                    
                    
            <div class="toast">
                <div class="toast__layout">
                    <span class="toast__icon fa--sm">
                        <i class="fas fa-exclamation-triangle"></i>
                    </span>
                    <span class="toast__text">
                        <strong>Warning: </strong>This is a warning alert
                    </span>
                    <button class="toast__close btn">
                        <i class="fa--sm fas fa-times"></i>
                    </button>
                </div>
            </div>
